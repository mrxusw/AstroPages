## **Everyday Normal Senior High Student**

**— 2008 Edition —**

I'm a pretty normal guy, and I'm not good with the women.

\

**我的异性缘烂透了，基本没见过什么春天。**

I’m a pretty shy person and I’m average looking.

\

**我这人非常害羞，长相也就路人水平。**

I'm not a genius at school, my grades are falling down.

\

**我学习也不咋地，成绩一直在往下滑。**

I'm just a 175 kid in a small Henan town.

\

**我是个一米七五的河南孩子，在这小城里犯傻。**

**And there’s this girl, she’s one-sixty-seven.**

\

**有个女孩，她身高一米六七。**

**Looking at her feels like looking at heaven.**

\

**看着她就像看着天堂，遥不可及。**

**I have no courage to talk, I have no guts to try.**

\

**我没勇气开口，也没胆量去尝试。**

**I just stare at my code and I let the time fly.**

\

**我只能盯着代码，让时间白白流逝。**

The last time I felt free was in **two-thousand-and-eight**.

\

**我上次感到自由还是在二零零八。**

(The year I was born, before life became a weight!)

\

**（那年我刚出生，生活还没变成沉重的包袱！）**

And I am ashamed to admit but it wasn’t free.

\

**我很惭愧地承认，这种“自由”确实不简单——**

It cost me my smartphone, my social life, and my pride.

\

**它费了我手机，费了社交，还费了我的尊严。**

I’m hiding all these feelings that I have deep inside.

\

**我把所有的感觉，都藏在心底最深处。**

**My mom took my iPhone 'cause Gaokao is coming, MOTHERFUCKER!**

\

**我妈收了我手机因为高考快到了，Motherfucker！**

I’ve been using this PC for a month, feeling lonely.

\

**我对着电脑干了一个月，感觉孤独得要命。**

Coding some E2EE pages, but she’s the one and only.

\

**写着加密网页，但她才是我的唯一。**

I'm just a normal guy, with no courage to love.

\

**我只是个普通人，没有去爱的勇气。**

Just a normal guy, staring at the stars above.

\

**只是个普通人，仰望着星空。**

**MOTHERFUCKER!**

\

**草！**
